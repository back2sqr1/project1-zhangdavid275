SERVICE

Apart from living online all the time, David is a large influence within the Texas A&M Singing Cadets, a men’s choir at Texas A&M of which he is extremely proud to be a part of. Singing at many university wide events from donation ceremonies to country music stars like George Strait, it surprises him to this day that he was able to find such a great group to engage in the arts in. He was last year’s social chairman, and this year helps the organization through event planning, often called “flower boy” due to his connections with florists in the Houston area.
